Ninja Run

This is a stealth based game.
The player must navigate the level without alerting the guards.
The guards will become alerted when their vision cone detects a collision with the player.
The player may use disguises, hiding spots and computer systems to help in avoiding the guards.

The controls

The up arrow key/ W will move the player forwards
The up arrow key/ S will move the player backwords
The up arrow key/ A will move the player to the left
The up arrow key/ D will move the player to the right
The spacebar allows the player to pick up items
I will be used to check for hiding place
U will be used to don a disguise
H will be used to utilise security systems

The camera is fixed above the player.
